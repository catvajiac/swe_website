Fall 2015: Adrienne Bruggeman

Adrienne Bruggeman is a 3rd year student in Saint Mary's 4:1 Engineering program. She studies chemistry at Saint Mary's and environmental engineering at Notre Dame. Adrienne is this years Saint Mary's - Notre Dame SWE Liaison and is currently working to improve communications between the campuses. When she is not studying or biking between campuses, Adrienne likes to keep busy as a second year Resident's Advisor in Le Mans Hall (she argues the best dorm on campus), as secretary and second violin performer for the Notre Dame Symphonic Orchestra, and as a laboratory assistant for an organic biogeochemistry laboratory at Notre Dame. Adrienne is particularly interested in designing for sustainability and in building a community of impact-aware citizens.
